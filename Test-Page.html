Tools and resources for creating a comprehensive OHS management system compliant with South African legislation and ISO 45001.

Introduction to OHS System

This flagship module empowers you to build a tailored Occupational Health and Safety (OHS) management system that meets South African laws (e.g., OHS Act 85 of 1993, COIDA) and international standards like ISO 45001. Explore our interactive tools to generate customized policies, procedures, and forms while learning the "why" behind each step.

Key Legal Obligations

Understand the legal foundations of your OHS system. Expand for details:

Section 7 of the OHS Act

The CEO must sign a Health and Safety Policy, setting the tone for safety commitment.

Section 8 of the OHS Act

Employers are obligated to ensure a workplace free from health and safety risks.

ISO 45001 Clause 6.1.2

Identify hazards and assess risks to proactively manage safety.
