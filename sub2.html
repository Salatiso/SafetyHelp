Risk Assessment

The Risk Assessment module helps you identify, evaluate, and mitigate workplace hazards in compliance with South Africa’s Occupational Health and Safety Act (OHS Act).

Key Legal Obligations

Hazard Identification

Employers must identify all hazards in the workplace to ensure a safe working environment.

OHS Act, Section 8
Risk Evaluation

Assess the risks associated with each identified hazard to determine their severity and likelihood.

OHS Act, Section 9
Control Measures

Implement appropriate control measures to mitigate identified risks.

OHS Act, Section 10
