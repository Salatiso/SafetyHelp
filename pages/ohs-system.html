OHS System

Tools and resources for creating a comprehensive OHS management system compliant with South African legislation and ISO 45001.

Introduction to OHS System

This module enables you to create a comprehensive Occupational Health and Safety (OHS) management system that complies with South African OHS legislation, such as the Occupational Health and Safety Act 85 of 1993 and COIDA, as well as international standards like ISO 45001. Use our interactive tools to generate customized policies, procedures, and forms for your company.

Key Legal Obligations

Below are key legal obligations for establishing an OHS management system. Click to expand for details.

Section 7 of the OHS Act

The CEO must sign a Health and Safety Policy for the company.

Section 8 of the OHS Act

Employers must provide and maintain a safe workplace free from risks.

ISO 45001 Clause 6.1.2

Organizations must identify hazards and assess risks.
