Welcome to the SafetyHelp OHS Community

Join a vibrant community dedicated to occupational health and safety (OHS) in South Africa. Whether you're a professional, supplier, contractor, or part of the informal sector, SafetyHelp connects you with mentors, career opportunities, and resources to ensure safety for all. Schools, churches, and public entities are encouraged to register and access tools to promote safety awareness and compliance.
