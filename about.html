About SafetyHelp

Welcome to SafetyHelp, a platform born from my lifetime of informal practical experience combined with over 20 years of formal work in the health and safety industry. My expertise is reinforced by studies in OHS, Risk Management, Compliance, Law, and Computer Programming, among others. I'm Salatiso Lonwabo Mdeni, and I've dedicated my career to enhancing workplace and community safety—evolving from an era of manual, cumbersome tools restricted to professionals, to pioneering accessible solutions for all. My vision has always been to create a future where OHS tools are universally accessible, intelligently automated, seamlessly integrated with risk management systems, and enhanced by AI to foster safer environments everywhere.

With a lifetime of practical informal experience and extensive formal expertise spanning both private and public sectors—including key roles at Anglo Platinum, Metrorail, and Absa—and operating my own OHS and risk management consultancy since 2012, I've witnessed firsthand how effective safety practices transform workplaces and save lives. This diverse experience has provided me with unique insights into safety challenges across multiple industries and organizational scales. SafetyHelp is the culmination of this journey, inspired by my books, Safety First: The Essentials of OHS Plans and Safety First: Essentials for Your OHS Career Journey, to empower businesses, professionals, and communities worldwide.

My Journey in Health and Safety

My journey in risk management began in the rural Transkei, where daily life taught me practical resilience—walking long distances to school, fetching water from rivers, gathering wood from forests, shepherding livestock, and tending gardens. These humble beginnings, where manual labor was essential to survival, instilled in me a deep understanding of environmental hazards and necessary precautions.

My formal path toward safety leadership crystallized at Kiriyatswane Secondary School in Embalenhle, where the industrial emissions from a nearby Sasol plant sparked my curiosity about health and safety. This led me to pursue Environmental Health at Pretoria Technikon (now Tshwane University of Technology). Since beginning as a Junior Environmental Health Practitioner in East London in 2003, my career has spanned diverse sectors—mining, smelting, transport and rail, property management in banking & insurance, and ultimately risk management in telecommunications. Since 2012, I've augmented my corporate roles with consulting work in OHS and risk management, serving both employers and clients that include government departments like Health and Metrorail, as well as major corporations such as Anglo Platinum, Mine Investment Corporation, Liberty Group Properties, Ster-Kinekor, ABSA (former Barclays Group Africa), and Gyro Group (Telkom Subsidiary).

Our Core Values (SALATISO)

The values that guide SafetyHelp stem from my personal philosophy, embodied in the SALATISO framework—Safety, Assurance, Learning, Adaptability, Training, Integrity, Solutions, and Operations. These core principles are not just words but commitments that shape every aspect of our service delivery.

  • Safety: Prioritizing the well-being of every individual.
  • Assurance: Ensuring confidence through reliable safety practices.
  • Learning: Committing to continuous improvement and education.
  • Adaptability: Embracing change to meet evolving safety needs.
  • Training: Empowering users with knowledge and skills.
  • Integrity: Upholding honesty and ethical standards in all we do.
  • Solutions: Providing practical, innovative tools for safety challenges.
  • Operations: Streamlining processes for efficient safety management.
